# Founder Loom Script — Federal Launch Kit ($70)

Goal: a 2-minute, talking-head Loom that makes $70 feel like a no-brainer and
lands the "buy once, keep forever, it keeps growing" idea. Talk to one person,
not a crowd. Read it out loud twice before recording so it sounds like you, not
like a script. Em-dashes are written as plain breaks on purpose.

**Setup**
- Webcam + screen-share. Spend the first 20 seconds on your face, then share the
  folder on screen and scroll through the files while you talk.
- Looser is better than polished. One take with a stumble beats a robotic read.
- Paste the final Loom embed URL into `LOOM_EMBED_URL` in
  `app/startup-pack/page.tsx` when you're done.

---

## The 2-minute script (~300 words)

**[0:00 – On camera]**
Hey, quick one. If you run a small business and you've been circling federal
contracts without actually winning one, this is for you. I'm Andre. My partner
and I run capture for veteran-owned firms, and we built this kit out of the exact
files we use when a client pays us to do it.

**[0:15 – Start sharing the folder, scroll slowly]**
Here's the honest version of how this usually goes. You try to register on
SAM.gov and quit halfway. You find a solicitation, write a proposal nobody asked
for, and lose to the incumbent who's known the agency for a year. Then you guess
at pricing and hope. None of that is a talent problem. It's a paperwork and
timing problem.

**[0:40 – Keep scrolling, point at specific files]**
So this is 60 files. The SAM.gov walkthrough with a screenshot of every screen.
The Sources Sought playbook, which is how you get in front of the contracting
officer a year before the RFP. The capability statement template they actually
read. A price-to-win calculator so you stop guessing. Real files. Not a PDF of
motivational quotes.

**[1:05 – On camera, slow down here]**
Now here's the part I actually want you to hear. It's seventy bucks, once. Not a
subscription. And every time we add a new template or calculator, it lands in
your folder for free. The price goes up as the library grows. Yours doesn't.
You're not buying 60 files. You're buying a seat that keeps getting more valuable.

**[1:35]**
Seventy dollars is less than an hour of a consultant. People have told us one
template saved them a forty-thousand-dollar bid they never should've chased.

**[1:48 – Close]**
If it's not useful, reply to the receipt in seven days and we send the seventy
back. You keep what you downloaded. That's the whole deal. Grab it, open it
tonight, go win something.

Hey, if you're a small business chasing federal contracts and not winning yet,

---

## Delivery notes
- Say the price out loud ("seventy bucks") at least twice. People skim; they
  listen for the number.
- The strongest line is "you're buying a seat that keeps getting more valuable."
  Land it on camera, not over the screen-share.
- End on an action ("open it tonight"), not a thank-you.
